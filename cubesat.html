Weather Forecasting Based on CubeSat

Project Details

Designed and developed a real-time weather forecasting CubeSat system using Arduino Uno. The CubeSat collects live environmental data such as temperature, humidity, rainfall, wind speed, and atmospheric pressure and transmits it to a ground station via LoRa communication.

The system integrates a GSM module to send SMS alerts to users in remote regions and a buzzer alert mechanism for instant local warnings during critical weather conditions. Powered by solar energy, the solution is sustainable, low-cost, and reliable.

Uses & Applications

  • Provides timely weather alerts to rural, hilly, and tribal areas without internet
  • Supports agriculture planning and disaster preparedness
  • Useful for CubeSat, IoT, and environmental research applications
  • Low-cost and scalable solution for real-time monitoring
